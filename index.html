App Features

  • Authentication

    Authentication on the cloud. Less overhead on your mobile device CPU.

  • Places Libarary Integrated

    Search for over 1,000+ relevant POIs seamlessly from within the app.

  • Network friendly

    POI is light and fluid. All images and data is pre optimized to load on an average internet connection efficiently.

  • Raw, Organic Data

    Raw, organic data. All that is necessary to help you explore and navigate around.

  • Seamless Booking

    Booking movie tickets and HRTC buses is directly integrated into the App. No external launches.

  • GPS support

    POI directly uses your GPS hardware to give you the most accurate location data possible.
